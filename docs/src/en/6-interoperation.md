# Interoperation

CSManager can exchange crystal data with related software such as PDIndexer and ReciPro.

## Automatic transfer on selection

When you select a crystal in the [Database table](2-database-table.md), if PDIndexer or ReciPro is running, the crystal's information is automatically transferred via the clipboard. Just by choosing a crystal in CSManager, you can load the same crystal into the target software.

You can also send the current crystal by right-clicking the [Crystal information](4-crystal-information/index.md) and choosing `Send this crystal to other software`.

## Importing crystal lists

From `Import PDI Crystal data (*.xml)` in the `File` menu, you can read a PDIndexer or ReciPro crystal list (`*.xml`) (see [Import and export](5-import-export.md)).
